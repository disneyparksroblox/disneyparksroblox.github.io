Frequently Asked Questions

What areas are open?

Currently, Buena Vista Street, Main Street, the Esplanade, and the hub are open to visitors, though more areas are in development.  Please note that some of these open areas are incomplete.

Do you take ride requests?

No, we currently don't take requests for rides and it's unlikely that we will.

What rides do you have?

We currently have the Red Car Trolleys on Buena Vista Street and the Horse-Drawn Streetcars on Main Street.  More attractions coming soon.

Is the game decorated for special events and holidays?

Yes, DPR decorates for it's own anniversaries, some special events from the parks, Halloween, and Christmas.

Does DPR intend to ever recreate attractions that are no longer at the Resort?

No, DPR is intended to be a recreation of the parks' current state.
